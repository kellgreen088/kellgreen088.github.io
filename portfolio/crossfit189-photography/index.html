CrossFit 189

Photography

I've owned a Nikon D7000 for a fair few years, and had been waiting for the 'right' opportunity to really dig in and learn how to use it properly. After a couple of online and in person classes, I grasped enough of the basics to really get started!

The more I learned, the more I knew that I really wanted to master photographing movement in particular - my home away from home CrossFit 189 was naturally the perfect place to get started; I'm inspired daily by the people I train with, our coaches and the community that the owners John and Sarah have created.

Once a year, CrossFit HQ holds an online competition named The Open. It acts almost like a right of passage for anyone participating in the sport as the workouts become benchmarks either on a personal level to gauge where you're at, or to see where you sit competing with the rest of the world!

The photos on this page were taken throughout the 2020 Open, as well as a few coaching clinics the box has held.
